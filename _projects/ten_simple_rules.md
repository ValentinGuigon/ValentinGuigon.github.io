  - Gaurav Mahajan and I are formalizing the infrastructure we built for the UMD Social Learning and Decisions Lab into a methodological manuscript on computational reproducibility in neuroscience.
  - The paper argues that reproducibility depends on the continuity of the full computational pathway, which includes inputs, decisions, environments, execution steps, outputs, and handoffs. We focus on low-cost practices that can be adopted early and scaled as projects become more complex, including project structure, version control, documentation, reusable code, stable environments, automation, bounded AI-assisted work, standardized data conventions, preprocessing and QC traceability, and lab-level defaults.
